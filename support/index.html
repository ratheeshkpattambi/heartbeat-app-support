HeartBeat Sound Lab

Support

HeartBeat Sound Lab is an educational audio-processing demonstration. It is not a medical device and must not be used for medical decisions or health monitoring.

Why does the app need microphone access?

The microphone provides sound samples for the live waveform and audio-filter demonstration. Samples are processed on the device and are not uploaded.

Why is no signal shown?

Open iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone and confirm microphone access is enabled for HeartBeat Sound Lab. Then try again in a quiet environment.

Why do I hear feedback?

Move the device farther from the sound source or reduce the speaker volume. Stop monitoring immediately if the sound becomes uncomfortable.

Is the BPM estimate medically accurate?

No. It is an experimental educational output and is not medically validated.
