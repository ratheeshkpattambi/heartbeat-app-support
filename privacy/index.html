HeartBeat Sound Lab

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 7, 2026

HeartBeat Sound Lab is an educational audio-processing application developed by Ratheesh Kalarot.

Information We Collect

HeartBeat Sound Lab does not collect personal information.

The app requests microphone access so it can process audio while the user has started monitoring. Microphone samples are processed locally on the device. They are not recorded to permanent storage, transmitted to the developer, sold, or shared with third parties.

The app stores one preference on the device indicating whether the educational and medical disclaimer has already been acknowledged.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

The app does not include advertising, analytics, tracking, user accounts, or third-party data-collection SDKs.

Medical Information

The app is for education and entertainment only. It is not a medical device and must not be used for diagnosis, treatment, medical decisions, or health monitoring.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated when the app's features or data practices change. The effective date above identifies the latest version.
